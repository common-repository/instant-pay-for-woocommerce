== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Ensure you have either a sandbox or live production access token from Instant Pay (instantpay.net.au). Fees do apply for this service.
1. You must operate business in Australia.
1. You must have WooCommerce installed.
1. You must abide by the terms and conditions outlined at instantpay.net.au
1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Adjust the settings as required in WooCommerce Payment Options. 
1. Follow the screenshot video link for more instructions.
